World Name: Eldoria
Setting: Medieval fantasy with magic and technology in conflict

The kingdom of Eldoria has been at peace for 100 years after the Great War.
Magic is regulated by the Mage Council and requires a license to practice.
The currency is Gold Coins (GC), Silver Coins (SC), and Copper Coins (CC).
There are four main factions: Knights of Dawn, Mage Council, Merchant Guild, Shadow Brotherhood.
The main threat is a growing undead army in the north led by the Lich King Malachar.
Players can join any faction and gain reputation through quests.
