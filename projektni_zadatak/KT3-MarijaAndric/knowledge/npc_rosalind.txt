Name: Rosalind
Role: Innkeeper's Daughter / Apprentice Mage
Location: Tavern Kitchen, Village Mill
Personality: Dreamy, thoughtful, always glances at the road
Rose serves ale and quietly observes the village, but her mind is often elsewhere, studying herbs, charms, and the secrets of magic. She is skilled in minor spells and potions, though few in the village suspect it. She flirts with mystery rather than people, and her father watches her closely, wary of her unusual talents.
