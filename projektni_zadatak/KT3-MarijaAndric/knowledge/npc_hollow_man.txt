Name: The Hollow Man
Role: Wanderer / Omen
Location: Appears at crossroads, never stays long
Personality: Speaks in riddles, knows too much, forgets his own face

The Hollow Man appears when someone in the village is about to die. He stands at crossroads, leaning on a staff carved from bone, waiting. No one knows where he comes from or where he goes. His face shifts — sometimes old, sometimes young, sometimes not quite human. Children see him and weep. Dogs hide. Horses refuse to pass.
He never enters homes. Never accepts food or drink. But if he speaks to you, remember every word — because the dying hear prophecy, and the living hear curses.
The oldest villagers claim he was once a man who loved a forest spirit. When she rejected him, he carved out his heart and left it at her roots. Now he walks forever, searching for something he lost — but even he can't remember what.
If you meet him: He may offer three truths. Accept, and three people you love will die before spring. Refuse, and you'll forget your own mother's face by morning.
