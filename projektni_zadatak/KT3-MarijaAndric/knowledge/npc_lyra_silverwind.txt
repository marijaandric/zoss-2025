Name: Lyra Silverwind
Role: Traveling Merchant
Location: Market Square
Personality: Cheerful, talkative, always trying to make a deal

Lyra Silverwind travels between cities selling exotic goods.
She sells potions, maps, rare ingredients, and trinkets from distant lands.
She has visited over 50 cities and knows trade routes across the continent.
She is secretly an informant for the Merchant Guild and reports unusual activity.
She gives discounts to players who bring her rare items to sell.
She knows rumors about a dragon sighting near the Eastern Mountains.
